About Waste Segregation

Waste segregation is a critical process in waste management that involves the separation of waste materials into different categories. This process helps to reduce the negative environmental impact caused by the improper disposal of waste. However, manual waste segregation can be a tedious and time-consuming process, leading to a lack of efficiency and accuracy. To address this issue, this website that utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to classify waste images into six categories: cardboard, glass, metal, paper, plastic, and trash.
